    Architecture¶

    amaru-treasury-tx is a thin executable on top of the cardano-node-clients TxBuild DSL. The behavioural source of truth is the bash recipe set in pragma-org/amaru-treasury/journal/2026/.

    Subcommand → module map¶

    Subcommand Drives
    swap-wizard Registry.Verify → Tx.SwapWizard → Tx.SwapIntentJSON (encode)
    swap Tx.SwapIntentJSON (decode) → Tx.SwapBuild → Tx.Swap
    disburse Tx.Disburse
    withdraw Tx.Withdraw

    Both swap-wizard and swap route every value-affecting step through a typed Tracer — WizardEvent and SwapEvent respectively. See the Trust model page for the full account of what flows through each event and what the operator must assert vs. what the verifier rejects.
